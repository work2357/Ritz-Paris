Dear Guests, Dear Friends,

The Ritz Paris is open and, as always, our teams are on hand to respond to your inquiries. During your stay, you may take full advantage of our delicious room service menu as well as access to the pool by reservation and in keeping with current health guidelines.

Need a little pampering? Don’t hesitate to book a treatment at the Ritz Club or an appointment with our star hairstylist, David Mallett. Gift cards are also available at the hotel or online, so you can surprise and delight loved ones by offering them a unique moment at the Ritz Paris. The Tasaki and TWG boutiques in our shopping gallery are open and pleased to welcome

guests wishing to stop by to see what’s new for the holidays, as well as our Concept Store which will welcome you every day from 11am to 6pm, until December 24th. We look forward to welcoming you once again to our bars an d restaurants in January 2021, as soon as health and safety regulations permit.

Most of all, we thank you for your confidence and wish you a happy and healthy holiday season.

Take good care,

The teams at the Ritz Paris
